#music #practice

Picking the guitar back up. The thing that moves the needle is short daily practice,
not marathon weekend sessions — fifteen focused minutes beats two hours once a week.
Start with the open chords (G, C, D, E minor, A minor) and drill clean changes
between them with a metronome, slow enough to be perfect, then nudge the tempo. Build
calluses gradually; sore fingertips are normal at first. Learn a few songs you
actually love early, so the practice feels like playing. Scales and theory come later;
momentum comes from making music now.

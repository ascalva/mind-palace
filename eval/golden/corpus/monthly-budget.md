#money #budgeting

How I think about the monthly budget. Pay myself first: move a fixed amount into
savings the day income lands, before any spending. Build the emergency fund to three
to six months of essential expenses, kept in a separate account so it's out of sight.
Track where the money actually goes for a month — the leaks are never where I guess.
Cut the recurring subscriptions I forgot I had. Automate the boring transfers so
discipline doesn't depend on willpower. Every windfall: split between the fund and
the things that matter, none of it absorbed silently into lifestyle creep.

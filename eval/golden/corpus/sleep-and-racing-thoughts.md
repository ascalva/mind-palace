#sleep #anxiety

Lying awake again past 3am with my mind racing through tomorrow's worries. What
actually helps me settle: slow box breathing — four counts in, hold four, four out —
and naming the fear out loud instead of letting it loop silently. Keeping the room
cool and dark matters more than I admit, and putting the phone across the room so I
can't doom-scroll. When I get up, write the loop down on paper and come back; the
page holds it so my head doesn't have to. Caffeine after noon wrecks the whole night.

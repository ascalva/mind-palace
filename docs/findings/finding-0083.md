# TRA §2.5's gloss "the owner is the only energy source" is too strong — dreaming is necessary

## What
`dn-temporal-retrieval-algebra` §2.3 Result 6 states the theorem correctly (the active-view
transport is a strict γ-contraction except at owner promotions) but its GLOSS — *"the owner is
the only energy source in the dynamics"* — is too broad. Owner correction (2026-07-15,
`temporal-clocks-and-strata.md` third capsule): the autonomous **dreamer is a second prime
mover** — it continuously drives the INTERPRETED/exploratory layer and keeps the system
fluctuating between owner touches; **dreaming is necessary, not optional**. The precise form
(fable pass T4): in the **belief view there is exactly one injection channel** (owner
promotions — the theorem stands unweakened); the dreamer **modulates that channel's rate and
quality** and drives the interpreted layer in a one-way-coupled two-layer skew-product, the
coupling being the promotion gate.

## Why it matters
The gloss is load-bearing downstream: V6 (prediction-residual = "the owner's perturbation") and
the T3/T4 curvature closure ("the owner's additions are the gravitational source") both inherit
it. Uncorrected, a diachronic instrument would mis-attribute dreamer-driven innovation to the
owner, and the heat-death analysis would wrongly predict stagnation between owner touches even
with the dreamer running. The corrected form also makes the demon-vs-source question a specified
experiment (provenance-decomposed innovation, fable pass V6.3).
